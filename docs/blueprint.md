# **App Name**: Birla Infra Projects - MEP Solutions

## Core Features:

- Responsive Layout: A fully responsive design that adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes (desktop, tablet, mobile) for optimal viewing experience.
- Services Showcase: Display core services (Tailored Projects, Efficient MEP Systems, Dedicated Support) with detailed descriptions and high-quality imagery.
- Project Portfolio: Showcase completed projects with images, descriptions, and client testimonials to demonstrate expertise and reliability.
- Contact Form Integration: A user-friendly contact form to facilitate inquiries and lead generation.
- FAQ Accordion: An accordion-style FAQ section addressing common queries related to MEP advantages, client support, and service overview.
- Value Proposition Grid: Clearly present Birla Infra Projects' key differentiators, emphasizing Engineering Quality, Client-Centric approach, and MEP Expertise.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Navy Blue (#232931) to convey professionalism and reliability.
- Background color: Slate Grey (#55626E) to provide a modern, industrial feel with a touch of sophistication.
- Accent color: Safety Orange (#E2725B) for Call-to-Action buttons and highlights, adding a sense of urgency and visibility.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean, bold, and highly readable typeface.
- Sticky header navigation to maintain consistent access to key site areas. Mobile-responsive 'hamburger' menu.
- Use minimalist icons representing services and values.
- Subtle page transitions for smooth navigation.
